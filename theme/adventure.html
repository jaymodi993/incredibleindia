Adventure

India’s dramatic topography, from lofty mountains to rushing rivers and from rugged deserts to sprawling beaches, promises you a potent adventure cocktail! Ski down the powdery slopes or go for a tiger safari, scuba dive in blue seas or take a bumpy desert camel ride- the options are various & varied. Whether you are an adventurer buff or a newbie with a bit of daring- India adventure tours are a perfect option for you to feed the adventurer in you!

Join us as we take you on an adventure spree through the stunning Indian landscape. With its numerous options, and each of them promising endless thrills, India is certainly the land of limitless adventure.

Trekking

With the towering Himalayas, Nilgiris and more sky-touching mountain ranges, trekking is the most preferred adventurous activity by the nature lovers in India. Himachal, Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Uttarakhand, Karnataka and North-East states are the top places offering paradisiacal spots for trekking like Triund trek, Chandrashila trek and Goecha La trek. Apart from walking in thick forests, you can also optimize your experience by choosing frozen river treks such as Chaddar trek in Jammu and Kashmir. Supernatural landscape places like these boast glacial lakes, perfect for spending a night under the sky full of stars camping by the lake.

Mountaineering, Trekking & Camping

Mountaineers & trekkers often say- “Mountains are calling!” And it certainly is true. While the newbies may begin with the Valley of Flowers Trek in Uttarakhand, the veterans may summit the majestic Nanda Devi or go for the precarious Kauri Pass Trek. Camp at spectacular campsites, encounter glacial lakes and give your body & spirit a chance to meld with Mother Nature.

White-Water Rafting

The swift alpine rivers of India, rushing through rapids, are just perfect for white-water rafting expeditions. The beginners may try the easier rapids on the Ganges near Rishikesh, and the veterans may feed their daredevil souls battling the rushing waters of Zanskar in Ladakh. Whatever grades of rapids you may choose, the fun is all the same. Our experienced rafting experts and quality gear ensure a safe & exhilarating rafting experience.

Skiing

Pick from the easier slopes in Auli & Narkanda to the higher gradients in Gulmarg & Pahalgam, for a delightful adventure in the snow. Brush your skiing skills with a quick course, rent the gear, and zoom down the rushing slopes on an exhilarating spree! Those not keen on skiing can always enjoy the cable car rides, pick a snowball fight or build your own snowman!

Jungle Safari

The sights & sounds of the forests, the thrilling wait, and the excitement of spotting a tiger or a herd of rhinos, are simply beyond words! From the tiger reserves in Ranthambore, Jim Corbett & Bandhavgarh, to the one-horned rhinos in Kaziranga, the colorful birds in Bharatpur and Chilika, the lions in Gir, and the elephants in Periyar- wildlife tours in India are just so exciting!

Desert Safari

Experience the rustic life of the desert nomads as you ride across the sands on a desert safari in the sprawling Thar Desert. Camp under the starlit skies, eat local cuisine, sit by the campfire amid folk music & dance, and feel the mystical allure of the desert. Desert Safaris in Bikaner, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer promise you a thrilling experience of the cultural & physical aspects of life in a desert.

Water Sports

Ride the waves on a surfboard in Kovalam, indulge in banana boat rides & water skiing in Goa, row a bamboo raft in Wayanad & Periyar, dig canoeing & kayaking in tidal pools or scuba dive in the coral waters of Andaman & Lakshadweep islands- its splashing fun all the way! Secure gear and supplies for water sports ensure a safe & happy experience.
