Darjeeling

Unshackle yourself from the restraints of stress and monotony by holidaying in the sublime hill-station of West Bengal, Darjeeling. Resting on the gradients of Himalayas, it will get you hooked to its entrancing beauty and renowned tea. Its sky-touching mountains will show you jaw-dropping views of Mt Everest and Kangchenjunga.

Potter around to get deep into the city; see the local life on the streets and gorge into steaming Momos and Thukpa. Don’t miss the unique experience of riding the Toy train and relish the marvelous views passing by the window.

Alluring Destinations of Darjeeling

Tiger Hill

It is a staggering 2,567 m high hilltop point famous for rendering panoramic view of Mount Everest and Mount Kangchenjunga together. Lying 11 km from Darjeeling, you can reach here by taking a jeep or trekking through Chowrasta, Alubari or Jorebangla. The best time to visit is early in the morning to catch a breathtaking sunrise view.

Darjeeling Toy train

One of the unmissable experiences of Darjeeling is its Toy train. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site also known as Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. Hop in this gauge railway that runs between Ghum and Darjeeling. The journey is fascinating and will keep you gripped to window views of scenic mountains.

Batasia Loop

The loop falling on the route of Toy Train makes the train ride experience more exciting. It is a beautiful park-like railway track loop beautified with colorful flowers. On a clear sky day, you can expect the majestic view of Kangchenjunga while passing through the loop.

Happy Valley Tea Estate

Your visit to Darjeeling is incomplete if you haven’t seen its world-famous tea plantations. Visit this one of the best tea estates in the city for the top-class experience. Sit in the cafe and sample the huge and flavorsome varieties of Darjeeling tea. The overlooking mountain views add to its beauty and will make you gaze at them forever.

Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (HMI) and Darjeeling Zoo

As the name suggests, it is a duo of a museum and a zoo. The museum was established in the memory of mountaineer Tenzing Norgay. Enter and find yourself surrounded by fascinating mountaineering gears and expedition artifacts. Next, visit the zoo and get introduced to thrilling Himalayan wildlife seeing animals like red panda, Tibetan wolf and snow leopard.

Best Time to Visit Darjeeling

Darjeeling is best visited in winters from October to March.

How to reach

By Air- Bagdogra Airport is the closest airport, located 90 km away from Darjeeling.

By Rail- New Jalpaiguri/Siliguri is the last railhead; thereafter you can board the narrow-gauge toy train to reach Darjeeling.

By Road- Darjeeling shares road connectivity with Siliguri, Bagdogra, Gangtok and Kathmandu.
